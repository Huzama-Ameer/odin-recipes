Ingredients

  • Starch 1/2 cup
  • Flour 1 1/2 cup
  • Sugar 1 kg
  • Small cardamom 6-7
  • Walnut halves 1 cup
  • Almond 1 cup
  • Clarified Butter 1 1/4 kg

Directions

⊳Step 1

Add starch, flour and butter in a pan and mix well.

⊳Step 2

Add 3 ltr water and mix well and then cook on low flame until water boils.

⊳Step 3

Now add sugar and cook until is completely dissolved.

⊳Step 4

Take off from stove and sieve through a muslin cloth to another pan.

⊳Step 5

cover sieved mixture on cool place for overnight.

⊳Step 6

Next day, cook again on low flame till the color change and add butter slowly and cook for a while.

⊳Step 7

Now blend cardamom and add into the halwa. At last, sprinkle almond and walnut.

⊳Step 8

Let it cool and cut into pieces and store in airtight box.
